\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

When inference token budgets are no longer binding, the interesting
variable is \emph{discipline}: the contract between an autonomous
writing agent and the human operator that prevents the agent's free
parameters from drifting through the parts of the paper that
constitute its claims. \texttt{cursor\_manager}'s four discipline
mechanisms target the four failure modes separating benchmark-grade
from submission-grade output. The system, the sub-agent contract,
this paper's source, and its full commit history are released under
MIT license (Appendix~\ref{app:repo}).
